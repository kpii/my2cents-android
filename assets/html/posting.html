Posting comments

To post comments press the Comment button at the bottom of the comments list. my2cents then displays a text input field. Type in your comment about the product and post it on Twitter.

my2cents offers a list of popular hashtags below the text input field. Touching on a hashtag adds it to the comment's text. The barcode number is automatically added as a hashtag.

You need to set your Twitter username and password in the my2cents preferences to be able to post comments. You can access the preferences by pressing the menu button of your phone.

Please note that your comments can only be seen in my2cents searches if your Twitter posts are public. You can set your Twitter account to public or private in the Twitter preferences.
