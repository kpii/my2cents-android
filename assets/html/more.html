About my2cents

my2cents is a mobile phone application for sharing comments on products. Use my2cents to read other people's opinions and remarks about products you are interested in. You can also post your own comments, either anonymously or using your Twitter account.

Login with your Twitter account to link your comments with your Twitter screen name and avatar picture. You can also choose to post your comments to your Twitter stream in the settings.

my2cents uses your phone's camera as a barcode scanner to identify products. Find out more about scanning and product barcodes:

  • How to scan product barcodes
  • About product barcodes

Also see http://my2cents.mobi for more information.

my2cents is developed at the Chair of Information Management at ETH Zürich, Switzerland
