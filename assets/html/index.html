Welcome to my2cents

my2cents uses the phone camera to scan product barcodes. When the product is recognized, comments from other users are displayed.

You can use my2cents to look at other people's opinions and experiences with products you are interested in. You can also post comments, either anonymously or using Twitter. After you login with your Twitter account your comments will be visible for others together with your Twitter name and picture. You can also publish your comments to your Twitter stream.
